BharatCric — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BharatCric ("we", "our", "us", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use the BharatCric mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you create a BharatCric account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Display name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Bio (optional)

b) Match and Cricket Data

When you use the App for scoring or playing cricket, we collect:

  • Match details (teams, players, venue, overs, scores, ball-by-ball events)
  • Player statistics (runs, wickets, strike rate, etc.)
  • Team information you create
  • Match invitations sent or received

c) Quick Scoring Data

If you use Quick Scoring without an account, your match data is stored on our servers anonymously and may be auto-deleted after 18 hours of inactivity.

d) Device and Usage Information

  • Device type, operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs (only if you opt in via Android system settings)
  • Anonymous analytics about feature usage

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the cricket scoring service
  • Show you live matches, your match history, and player statistics
  • Allow other users to find you via username search
  • Display match results and scorecards to viewers
  • Send you match invitations when other users invite you
  • Improve the App and develop new features
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or illegal activity

3. Information Sharing

Match data you create as a host or co-host is publicly viewable within the App. This includes:

  • Match scores and ball-by-ball history
  • Team names and player names
  • Match results and statistics

Your profile information (display name, username, profile photo, bio, public stats) is visible to other BharatCric users.

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share information:

  • With service providers who help us operate the App (e.g., Google Firebase for authentication, database, and storage)
  • If required by law or in response to legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of BharatCric, our users, or the public

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers. Firebase provides industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Match data (profile matches): Retained while the match is referenced or up to 90 days after completion
  • Quick Scoring matches: Auto-deleted 18 hours after inactivity
  • Inactive matches: Auto-stopped after 1 hour of inactivity and deleted after 18 hours

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account data through the Profile screen
  • Edit your profile name, username, bio, and photo at any time
  • Delete teams you created
  • Delete matches you hosted
  • Delete your account by contacting us at the email below

7. Children's Privacy

BharatCric is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it.

8. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera/Photos: To upload a profile picture and team logos
  • Internet: Required for all online features

You can revoke any permission at any time via your Android device settings.

9. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore Database, Cloud Storage). Use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date or via an in-app notice.
